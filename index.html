Most homes benefit from power washing once a year. However, if you live in an area with high humidity, near the ocean, or with lots of trees, you might need cleaning every 6-9 months to prevent mold, mildew, and algae buildup.

While power washing is generally safe for most exterior surfaces, some delicate materials like older brick, certain types of wood, and some roof shingles require special care. Our professionals adjust the pressure and technique based on each surface to ensure safe, effective cleaning.

The main difference is that power washing uses heated water, which is more effective at removing tough stains, grease, and grime. Pressure washing uses unheated water at high pressure. We use the appropriate method based on your specific cleaning needs.
